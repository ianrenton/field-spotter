Using the tool

POTA and SOTA spots from the last hour are displayed on the map. Click a marker for details.

The button allows you to filter by mode and band.

The button takes the spots currently on the map and shows where they are on the bands as well.

You can install Field Spotter on your phone! This is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means on Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS) there should be an "Install" option in the menu. This will add Field Spotter to your home screen. An internet connection is still required to retrieve the latest data.

Privacy

This website collects no data about you. If you share your location, the information stays on your device. There are no trackers and no cookies.
